Our Mission

Urdu is spoken by over 230 million people worldwide, yet it remains underrepresented in AI and NLP research. Our project bridges this gap by developing specialized ML models for Urdu speech analysis. We focus on real-time stress detection, emotion classification, and deception analysis — tools that can help in mental health screening, interview analysis, and security applications.

Our Vision

We envision a world where Urdu language technology is as advanced as English or Chinese. Our goal is to create open-source tools and models that empower developers, researchers, and businesses to build Urdu-first applications. From healthcare to education to security, we believe AI should serve all languages equally.
