About Us

We are building the future of Urdu speech analysis using cutting-edge AI and machine learning technology. Our mission is to make Urdu language processing accessible, accurate, and impactful.

Our Mission

Urdu is spoken by over 230 million people worldwide, yet it remains underrepresented in AI and NLP research. Our project bridges this gap by developing specialized ML models for Urdu speech analysis.

We focus on real-time stress detection, emotion classification, and deception analysis — tools that can help in mental health screening, interview analysis, and security applications.

What We Do

Our system processes Urdu speech through a sophisticated pipeline: audio input → feature extraction (64 acoustic features) → ML classification (XGBoost) → stress/emotion/lie detection → Urdu transcription via OpenAI Whisper.

With 67%+ accuracy on a 6-emotion classification task and real-time processing, we deliver practical results for real-world Urdu speech analysis.
