Frequently Asked Questions

What audio formats are supported?
We support WAV, MP3, FLAC, OGG, and WebM formats. For best results, use WAV files with 16kHz sample rate. Live recording is also supported directly in the browser.
How accurate is the emotion detection?
Our XGBoost model achieves approximately 67% accuracy on 6-emotion classification (angry, disgust, fear, happy, neutral, sad). Performance varies by emotion — neutral and angry tend to have higher recall, while subtle emotions like disgust are harder to detect.
Is my audio data stored permanently?
No. Audio files are processed in real-time and deleted from the server after analysis. Only the extracted features and analysis results are stored in your account history.
Can I use this for real-time monitoring?
Yes! The browser-based live recording feature allows real-time analysis. Simply click "Live Recording" on the Analyze page, record your voice, and get instant results.
How does the lie detection work?
Our lie detection uses a rule-based algorithm that combines the ML-detected emotion and stress level with acoustic feature analysis (pitch variation, zero-crossing rate, amplitude patterns). It provides a probability estimate, not a definitive verdict.
