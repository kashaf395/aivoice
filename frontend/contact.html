Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

What audio formats are supported?
We support WAV, MP3, FLAC, OGG, and WebM formats. For best results, use WAV files with 16kHz sample rate. Live recording is also supported directly in the browser.
How accurate is the emotion detection?
Our XGBoost model achieves 98% accuracy on 6-emotion classification (angry, disgust, fear, happy, neutral, sad). Performance varies by emotion type.
Is my audio data stored permanently?
No. Audio files are processed in real-time and deleted from the server after analysis. Only the extracted features and results are stored in your account.
Can I use this for real-time monitoring?
Yes! The browser-based live recording feature allows real-time analysis. Simply click "Live Recording" on the Analyze page and get instant results.
How does lie detection work?
Our lie detection uses a rule-based algorithm combining ML-detected emotion, stress level, and acoustic feature analysis. It provides a probability estimate.
Is the service free to use?
We offer a free tier with limited daily analyses. Premium plans with unlimited access and advanced features are available for enterprise users.
